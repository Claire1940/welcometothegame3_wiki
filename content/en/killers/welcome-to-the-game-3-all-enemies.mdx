Not all enemies in **welcome to the game 3 all enemies** want to kill you immediately. Some, like **Cletus**, are interested in trade or extortion. Cletus is a unique NPC who resides in the surrounding motel rooms. To keep him from becoming hostile, you must always carry a "peace offering."

<Section icon={Target} title="Psychological Horrors: The Noir and Kidnapper">

The **Noir** represent the psychological toll of "The Game." They appear suddenly and challenge your ability to stay calm under pressure. There are two variants: a female and a male. Your reaction time and counting accuracy are the only things that will save you from an instant death.

| Noir Variant | Visual Cue | Required Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Female Noir** | Pale figure, long hair | Turn around, count to **10 seconds**, turn back |
| **Male Noir** | Tall, shadowed figure | Turn around, count to **5 seconds**, turn back |

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Timing">
Counting must be exact. Turning back too early or too late will result in a jump scare and immediate death. Use the in-game clock or a real-world stopwatch if necessary.
</Callout>

The **Kidnapper** is a more traditional threat. He drives a white van and will occasionally park on the street before attempting to enter the motel. He is a high-speed threat that requires quick reflexes to lock him out or hide before he reaches your floor.

</Section>
