<Section icon={Terminal} title="Mastering the Noir and Bomb Maker">

The Noir and the Bomb Maker represent the more "abstract" threats among the **welcome to the game 3 all killers**. These entities don't always follow the physical rules of the motel, often attacking through the interface or through psychological manipulation.

**The Noir Twins**
The Noir appear as static-filled figures. They require a specific "Counting Counter" to defeat. If you see the female Noir, you must turn away and count to exactly 10 seconds before looking back. For the male Noir, the count is reduced to 5 seconds. Precision is key; turning back too early or too late will lead to a jump scare and death.

**The Bomb Maker**
This threat activates specifically when you visit "The Bomb Maker" website. He will plant a physical bomb in the motel area. You must locate the bomb and wait for a message containing the disarm code.
