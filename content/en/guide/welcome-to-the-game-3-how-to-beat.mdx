<Section icon={Shield} title="Threat Mitigation: Kidnapper & Noir">

The physical threats in this game are highly aggressive and will end your run instantly if ignored. You must split your focus between the computer screen and your physical surroundings. The two main antagonists require completely opposite defensive strategies, making audio cues and camera checks vital.

The Kidnapper operates via a white van that parks outside your building. You must monitor the security cameras regularly to spot his arrival. The Noir, on the other hand, is a stealthy stalker who creeps up behind you, often triggered if you leave the lights off for too long.

| Threat | Trigger Condition | Defensive Action | Danger Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Kidnapper** | Van appears in security cameras | Turn off both lights, wait for van to leave | **High** |
| **The Noir** | Lights left off too long | Stand perfectly still when backing out | **Extreme** |

Managing these threats requires precise timing. If you turn off the lights to hide from the Kidnapper, you must turn them back on as soon as the van leaves. Leaving the lights off for more than 30 seconds almost guarantees a visit from the Noir.
