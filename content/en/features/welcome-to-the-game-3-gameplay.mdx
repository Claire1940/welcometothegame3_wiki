### Defensive Checklist
- **Audio Cues**: If the ambient room sound changes (e.g., the rain gets louder), a window or door may have been opened.
- **Light Management**: Do not keep all lights on; it makes you an easy target. However, total darkness prevents you from seeing intruders. Find a balance.
- **Computer Security**: Always keep your security utility tab open in the background for quick access during a digital attack.

<Callout type="warning" title="The Distraction Trap">
