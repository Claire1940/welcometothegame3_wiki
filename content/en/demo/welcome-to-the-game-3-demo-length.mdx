The Kidnapper operates from a van outside. He doesn't know your exact location initially, but he drives around the neighborhood searching for light signatures. Patrick, on the other hand, is a more psychological threat who monitors your progress from within the room.

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Complete the Trial Run">

To successfully finish the Welcome to the Game 3 demo, you must follow the instructions provided by Ronald. The core loop involves finding hashes, which are long strings of alphanumeric characters hidden within the Darknet.

Finding these hashes is a multi-layered process. Some are **hidden in plain sight** on a webpage, often disguised as part of the text. Others are **hidden inside elements**, requiring you to click on specific words or images to reveal them. The most difficult hashes are tucked away in the **web page's source code**, which you must inspect manually.

| Task | Description | Reward |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Find Hash 1** | Located on the initial Wiki or secondary links | Progress to Decryption |
| **Find Hash 2** | Often requires viewing source code or clicking images | Final Key Component |
| **Hire Agent** | Pay Doscoin to an agent via Crypt Chat | Decrypted Key Fragment |
| **Compile Key** | Combine fragments by their index | Trial Completion |
