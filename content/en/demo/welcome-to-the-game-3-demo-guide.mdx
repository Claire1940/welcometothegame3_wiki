<Section icon={Shield} title="Threat Management & Survival Strategies">

Survival in this simulator requires acute spatial awareness. You are hunted by two primary physical threats: the Kidnapper and the Noir. Ignoring either of these entities will result in an immediate game over.

### The Kidnapper (The Van)
The Kidnapper patrols the surrounding neighborhood attempting to triangulate your exact location. He cannot pinpoint your position unless the sensors in your basement detect strong light emanating from within.

- **Detection**: Check your security cameras regularly via the Sic Cam application. Look for a suspicious white van parked on the street.
- **Countermeasure**: Immediately get up from your desk, walk to the light switches, and turn off both basement lights.
- **Resolution**: Return to your computer (you can still use the terminal in the dark) and monitor the cameras. Once the van drives away, immediately turn the lights back on to prevent other threats from spawning.

### The Noir
The Noir is a silent stalker who stands directly behind you. He is attracted to prolonged darkness, meaning leaving the basement lights off for too long will invite him into your room.

- **Detection**: When sitting at your computer, look at the wall directly behind your monitor. If you see a distinct human shadow cast on the wall, the Noir is present.
- **Countermeasure**: Do not get up from your chair, and do not attempt to turn around. You must remain completely still at your computer terminal.
- **Resolution**: Wait silently until the shadow completely disappears from the wall before resuming your tasks.

| Threat | Primary Trigger | Detection Method | Correct Response |
|---|---|---|---|
| Kidnapper | Active basement lights when the van is nearby | Security camera feed (Sic Cam) | Turn off all basement lights |
| The Noir | Prolonged darkness or random spawning | Shadow cast on the wall behind the PC | Stay seated and do not move |

<Callout type="warning" title="Beware of Audio Distractions">
All sound cues in the demo, including footsteps and background whispers, are completely fake and designed to induce panic. Rely strictly on visual confirmation via cameras and shadows to make your survival decisions.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Hacking Mini-Games & Defense Guide">

As you browse the darknet, rogue hackers will periodically attempt to breach your system. When a hack occurs, your screen will lock, forcing you to solve one of two security mini-games to prevent system failure and the loss of your hard-earned DOS Coins.

### Stack Pusher
A returning mechanic from previous entries. You must manipulate memory stacks on a grid.
- **How to play**: Move the cursor adjacent to the memory blocks and push them into the designated center zone.
- **Strategy**: Focus on the outer blocks first to prevent them from getting stuck against the grid borders.

### Memory Deallocator
A fast-paced, rhythm-style mini-game that requires precise inputs based on visual indicators.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Analyze the Memory Block">
    Focus your attention entirely on the last line displayed in the allocator interface.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify the Green Target Zone">
    Observe which section of the line is highlighted in green: the left side, the right side, or both sides.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Input the Corresponding Command">
    Press the Left Arrow key for the left side, the Right Arrow key for the right side, or the Spacebar if both sides are highlighted.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute with Rhythm">
    Complete the sequences line-by-line. Avoid button mashing, as incorrect inputs will instantly trigger a system breach.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Mini-Game | Input Controls | Focus Area | Failure Consequence |
|---|---|---|---|
| Stack Pusher | Directional Keys | Outer grid blocks | Loss of DOS Coins |
| Memory Deallocator | Left Arrow / Right Arrow / Spacebar | Bottom green line | System lock and threat acceleration |
