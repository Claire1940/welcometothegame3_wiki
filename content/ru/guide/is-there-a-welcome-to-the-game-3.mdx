# Введение в Welcome to the Game 3: Выживание в глубокой сети

Welcome to the Game 3 is a thrilling survival horror game that takes players into the depths of the dark web. In this guide, we'll cover the essential mechanics, strategies, and tips to help you survive and thrive in this challenging game.

## Введение

In Welcome to the Game 3, you play as Simon Xiao, a degenerate gambler working the night shift at a rundown motel. After borrowing money from the wrong person, you're forced to play "The Game" to clear your outstanding debt. The game is set in a modern-day setting, and you'll need to navigate the dark web, hack into PCs, and avoid various threats to survive.

## Основные механики

### Нavigating the dark web

One of the core mechanics of Welcome to the Game 3 is navigating the dark web. You'll have access to 52 websites where you can find information, hack into PCs, and buy/sell goods and services. Each website has its own unique layout and objectives, so it's important to be familiar with them.

### Hacking

Hacking is a crucial skill in Welcome to the Game 3. You'll need to hack into PCs to gather information, download files, and access hidden areas. There are multiple hacking minigames that test your reaction speed, typing ability, and puzzle-solving skills.

### Resource Management

Managing your resources is essential for survival. You'll need to gather keys, hack into PCs to get DOSS coins, and use them to buy upgrades and equipment. It's important to prioritize key discovery and resource management to ensure you have what you need to survive.

## Лучшая стратегия

### Приорitize Key Discovery

Finding the eight keys is crucial for clearing your debt and winning the game. Make sure to prioritize key discovery by exploring different websites and solving puzzles to uncover the keys.

### Stay Vigilant

Welcome to the Game 3 is filled with various threats, including hitmen, breathers, and other players. Stay vigilant at all times and be prepared to react quickly to avoid danger.

### Manage Your Inventory

Keep your inventory organized and secure. Use your DOSS coins to buy upgrades and equipment that can help you survive and complete the game.

## Советы и хитрости

### Use the Panic Button

The panic button is a useful tool for securing the front sliding door and stopping anyone from entering. Use it when you're in immediate danger or need to quickly secure the area.

### Keep Your Back Doors Locked

Always keep your back doors locked to prevent hitmen from sneaking up on you. Be careful when heading outside, as there may be hitmen waiting in the surrounding areas.

### Learn from Your Mistakes

Welcome to the Game 3 is a challenging game, and you'll make mistakes along the way. Learn from your mistakes and use them to improve your strategy for future runs.
